Welcome to Ecofest where we combine music and recycling ideas. Ecofest will include acts such as Everyon's Environment, Solar Drum, Green Gizmos and DJ John Alfred. The aim of Ecofest is to introduce a music festival that is Eco friendly by recyling rubbish from the festival.
